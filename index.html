About Us

Leading Dialysis & Diagnostic Center

At Brilliant Healthcare, we are dedicated to providing exceptional medical care with a focus on dialysis treatment and advanced diagnostic services. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology, and our team of experienced specialists is committed to delivering personalized care to every patient.

Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Services

From routine check-ups to specialized dialysis care, we offer a full range of medical services under one roof.

HD

Hemodialysis

Advanced hemodialysis treatments using state-of-the-art machines to effectively filter waste and toxins from your blood.

PD

Peritoneal Dialysis

Flexible peritoneal dialysis options with comprehensive training and support for home-based treatment.

LAB

Lab Diagnostics

Full-service diagnostic laboratory offering accurate blood tests, urine analysis, and specialized pathology services.

ECG

Cardiac Diagnostics

Comprehensive cardiac testing including ECG, echocardiograms, and stress tests for heart health assessment.

IMG

Radiology & Imaging

Digital X-ray, ultrasound, and other imaging services to support accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

HC

Health Checkups

Comprehensive health screening packages designed to detect health issues early and promote wellness.
